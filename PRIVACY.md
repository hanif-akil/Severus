# Privacy Policy for Severus Keyboard

**Last updated: July 2026**

## Overview

Severus Keyboard is an open-source Android keyboard application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about what data we collect.

## Data Collection

**Severus Keyboard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- We do not collect keystrokes or typing data
- We do not collect usage statistics
- We do not track your activity
- We do not use analytics services
- We do not display advertisements

## Clipboard

The clipboard history feature operates entirely on your device. Clipboard data is stored in memory only and is never:
- Saved to persistent storage
- Transmitted over the network
- Accessible to other applications

When you close the keyboard or clear the clipboard history, all stored clipboard items are permanently deleted from memory.

## Permissions

Severus Keyboard requires the following permissions:
- **VIBRATE**: Used for haptic feedback on key presses (optional)

## Network Access

Severus Keyboard does not require internet access and does not make any network connections.

## Children's Privacy

Severus Keyboard does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and posted to this repository.
